Types of Bicycles

The term road bicycle is used to describe bicycles built for traveling at speed on paved roads.

A mountain bike is a bicycle designed for off-road cycling, like mountain trails and single tracks.

A gravel bike is a drop-bar bike designed to let you ride over many different surfaces.

Commuter bikes are generally used for daily and study travel. They are reliable, comfortable and are easy to use.

Bicycles are great! They go from place to place in an efficient manner and leave no carbon footprint behind. Everyone should buy a bike!
